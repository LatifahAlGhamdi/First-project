Raghadan Forest Park

Location
Raghadan Forest Park is one of the most important tourism destinations in Saudi Arabia in general, and tourism in Al-Baha in particular. The park is characterized by its beautiful landscapes, thanks to its distinguished geographical location, at the top of the Raghadan Forest, where the green and desert nature of the surrounding mountain peaks harmonize; Together, they form a natural painting, whose beauty cannot be described, made by God Almighty.

Khairah Forest Park

Location
One of the most beautiful tourist areas in Al Baha and always open its doors; For nature lovers, the picturesque scenery, of vast green spaces, flowing waterfalls, and pools of water surrounded by picturesque rock formations. If you are looking for the best tourist places in Al-Baha, make Khaira Forest Park at the top of your list of choices, especially as it is equipped with all service facilities, such as restrooms, toilets, seating areas, exercise paths, and 3 sites for children's games. Khaira Forest Park is a beautiful natural painting that bears witness to the wonderful landmarks of Saudi Arabia, and its prominent tourist position.

Prince Hussam Park

Location
One of the most famous tourist places in Al-Baha for families; It is full of green spaces, fountains, artificial lakes, natural waterfalls, and other wonderful attractions. Prince Hussam Park is located in the center of the city of Al Baha, and gives visitors the most beautiful view of the city. For children's games, a walking path, cycling, a chapel for men and women, a train car with a capacity of 55 people, and a car park for 500 cars.

Dhee Ayn, Marble Village

Location
The history of the archaeological village of Dhi Ain dates back to the eighth century AD, and it is one of the most important (tourist) landmarks of Al-Baha. It is named for the standing water that flows through the mountains for the village. It is surrounded by mountains on three sides. The archaeological village of Dhi Ain is located on the highest mountain peak, on the coast 24 km, southwest of the city of Al-Baha, and it consists of 312 houses and one mosque. The houses of the ancient village of Ain are similar in terms of planning, having spent seven years against some cases, and built of polished stone. Visit this wonderful village, which is one of the most beautiful tourist places in Al-Baha, and enjoy the most wonderful views inside and the surrounding area.

Shada Mountain Reserve

Location
Tourist areas in Al-Baha give you a calm atmosphere, amidst the picturesque nature. There is the Shada Mountain Top Park as one of the most beautiful of those areas. This park is located southwest of the city of Al-Baha, the highest peak of Shada Mountain, its height is about 2200. Shada Mountain Park is considered one of the most beautiful tourist places in Al-Baha because of its rich green spaces, all kinds of trees, and palms, in addition to many wild animals, some of which are at risk of extinction, such as the Arabian leopard.

The Al Baha Museum is one of the most important tourist destinations in Al Baha.
